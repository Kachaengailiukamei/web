Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

By Avineet | 20 mins | Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Chilli flakes, salt, and parsley

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil and sauté garlic till golden.
  3. Add chilli flakes and toss in the pasta.
  4. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
